Know Your Rights

Every Kenyan citizen has constitutional rights. Learn them. Enforce them.

Art 35

Access to Information

Every citizen has the right to access information held by the State

Art 43

Economic & Social Rights

Right to health care, education, accessible housing, food & water

Art 47

Fair Administrative Action

Administrative action must be expeditious, efficient, lawful, reasonable

Art 201

Public Finance Transparency

Conducted with openness, accountability, public participation

Art 77

Leadership Integrity

State officers must declare income, assets, liabilities. No using power for personal gain
