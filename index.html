Civic Education

Know Your Rights

Every Kenyan citizen has constitutional rights. Learn them. Enforce them.

Art 35

Access to Information

Every citizen has the right to access information held by the State

Art 43

Economic & Social Rights

Right to healthcare, education, accessible housing, food & water

Art 47

Fair Administrative Action

Administrative action must be expeditious, efficient, lawful, reasonable

Art 201

Public Finance Transparency

Public finance conducted with openness, accountability, public participation

Art 77

Leadership Integrity

State officers must declare income, assets, liabilities. No using power for personal gain

⚠️ 48 Laws of Political Power — Spot Manipulation

Politicians use these tactics. Know them. Don't fall for them.

Law 3: Vague Promises
🔴 "I'll create jobs" — no plan, no budget, no timeline
✅ Ask: "What is your specific plan? Where's the budget?"
Law 8: Bribery
🔴 "Gifts" of maize, cash, food during campaign
✅ This is YOUR tax money. Real leaders provide systems, not handouts.
Law 27: Ethnic Division
🔴 "My tribe deserves to eat" / "If they win, you suffer"
✅ Demand policies for ALL. Constitution Art 27 says NO discrimination.
Law 43: Emotional Manipulation
🔴 Crying, anger, fear at rallies — zero policy details
✅ Separate feelings from facts. Ask for their track record.
